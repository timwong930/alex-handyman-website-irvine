# Alex Handyman Service website starter

## Public business snapshot
- **Company:** Alex Handyman Service
- **Location:** Irvine, CA
- **Category:** Handyman
- **Website:** https://alexhandyman.wixsite.com/home
- **Social:** none found in the public scan

## What they do
A solo handyman brand in Irvine offering carpentry and minor repair work, with a focus on quick household fixes.

## Why this looks like a good opportunity
- The site is on a Wix subdomain and still shows the built-on-Wix watermark text in the page copy.
- The structure is template-driven, with a generic “HOME | alexhandyman” title and limited custom branding.
- A custom domain, tighter service pages, and a cleaner quote form would make it look much more established.

## Suggested outreach / redesign angle
Pitch a custom-domain upgrade and a more professional Irvine brand that converts local traffic better on mobile.

## Starter site included in this repo
This repo includes a simple, responsive starter site in `index.html` and `styles.css` built only from public information.

### Draft structure
- **Carpentry** — Shelf building, trim touch-ups, and small repairs.
- **Closet solutions** — Simple organization and custom-fit storage updates.
- **Home fixes** — Minor repairs and one-off household tasks.
- **Contact** — A clear call button and quote form for quick inquiries.
